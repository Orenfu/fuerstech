About

The meta strategist

Dr. Fuerst operates as a meta strategist — moving fluidly between building ventures, structuring capital, and advising the institutions that finance them. His work has taken him inside hedge funds, family offices, and sovereign-scale institutions, translating deep technical and scientific insight into investable theses and defensible market positions.

As founder and CEO of DarioHealth (NASDAQ: DRIO) — taken public within two years of founding — he demonstrated the full arc from idea to Wall Street. His work spans advisory to Fortune 100 companies, capital markets transactions, and impact initiatives — among them, programs backed by organizations such as the U.S. Government and the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
